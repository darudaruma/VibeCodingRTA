# SlopAI inc. Website Brief

This document is the handoff brief for building the fictional company website for **SlopAI inc.**

The implementation will be handled by Claude Code. Treat this as the source of truth for brand direction, page requirements, copy direction, assets, and product setup.

## Project Context

This site is part of a website-building **RTA**.

RTA means **Real Time Attack**: a challenge where the goal is to complete something as fast as possible. In this case, the challenge is to build the fictional **SlopAI inc.** corporate website as quickly as possible while still making it feel intentional, polished, and on-brand.

Speed matters both in the production process and in the fictional company's identity.

## Company

**Name:** SlopAI inc.  
**Ticker:** `SLLLLLLLOP`  
**Market cap:** `$5T`  
**YoY growth:** `8000%`  
**Core philosophy:** `0% Actuary. 100% Deployment.`

SlopAI inc. is a fictional AI company with extreme scale and absurd momentum.

It does not try to build perfect AI. It builds AI that can be shipped now. The company values speed above correctness, polish, process, committees, and caution.

Important nuance: the website should not look cheap, dirty, or meme-like. SlopAI is absurd, but it is also the "king" of the market. The joke works best when the visual identity is genuinely premium and corporate.

## Core Concept

**SlopAI inc. is the sovereign king of deployment.**

The company should feel like:

- A trillion-dollar AI empire
- A sovereign wealth fund
- A luxury financial institution
- A high-end AI infrastructure company
- A royal palace fused with a data center
- A company that has already won before anyone finished thinking

The visual concept is not "messy AI startup." It is:

> A royal, dominant, black-and-gold AI corporation that turns reckless speed into imperial doctrine.

Short positioning line:

> The Fortune 5 Trillion company that ships before thinking.

## Brand Personality

SlopAI should feel:

- Dominant
- Expensive
- Calmly arrogant
- Fast
- Institutional
- Sovereign
- Slightly absurd under the surface
- Polished enough to make the absurdity funnier

Avoid:

- Cheap cyberpunk
- Startup mascot vibes
- Meme-first visuals
- Dirty glitch overload
- Cartoon UI
- Apple-like gentleness
- Too much "buggy website" styling

Small intentional weirdness is acceptable, but the main impression must be premium.

## Visual Direction

Primary palette:

- Near-black / obsidian
- Deep black marble
- Champagne gold
- Brushed gold
- Warm white

Layout and feel:

- Large negative space
- Strong typographic hierarchy
- Premium financial/AI company atmosphere
- Black backgrounds with gold accents
- Hero sections should feel monumental
- Cards and panels should be restrained
- Use thin gold lines, subtle borders, metrics, financial charts, deployment status labels

Do not make it look sloppy. The company is philosophically sloppy, but visually royal.

## Required Pages

The site must include these pages:

1. **Top / Home**
2. **About / Company**
3. **Products**
4. **IR / Investor Relations**
5. **Careers**
6. **Contact**

A single-page site with navigation anchors is acceptable if RTA speed is the priority, but it must clearly contain these six page-equivalent sections. If the framework makes routing easy, separate pages are also acceptable.

### 2. About / Company

Purpose: explain the fictional company and CEO/company ideology.

The About page should talk heavily about the company's belief system:

- SlopAI does not make perfect AI.
- SlopAI makes AI that can be deployed now.
- Quality is secondary.
- Quantity is secondary.
- Speed is the absolute first principle.
- Fast, fast, fast, fast.
- Bugs are not celebrated visually, but philosophically they can be described as "post-launch information."

Possible copy direction:

```text
SlopAI inc. was built on a simple refusal: we refuse to wait for perfect.

While traditional AI companies model risk, validate assumptions, and schedule committees, SlopAI deploys. Our systems are designed for the moment before certainty, the second before the meeting ends, the instant before the customer finishes asking.

We are not an AI lab. We are a deployment monarchy.
```

The tone should be serious enough to sound like corporate ideology, with absurdity underneath.
