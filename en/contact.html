Contact FAQ

Questions we've pre-answered.

How quickly will I receive a response?

SlopAI's response infrastructure operates pre-comprehension. You may receive a response before your inquiry has been fully read. This is the intended behavior and considered an SLA feature, not a defect.

What is an "Emergency Launch" inquiry?

An Emergency Launch inquiry signals that you need to ship something before any other stakeholder is aware that shipping is happening. Our Emergency Launch team deploys within 0 seconds. Review is not part of this process.

Can I request a product demo before purchasing?

You can request a demo. However, SlopAI typically begins the enterprise onboarding process before the demo is confirmed. By the time your demo is scheduled, you may already be deployed.

What information should I include in my message?

Include your deployment intent, budget posture, and approximate urgency. Exact details are not required — we will proceed based on available momentum and clarify afterward.

Is the form data reviewed before being processed?

No. Form data is processed and acted upon immediately. Review occurs, if at all, post-action. This is consistent with SlopAI's operating model across all functions.

What happens after I submit?

A response is dispatched. An account may be created. A contract may be drafted. You will be informed of each step once it has already occurred.
