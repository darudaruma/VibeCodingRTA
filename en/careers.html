Who We Hire

We hire for velocity,
not for caution.

SlopAI does not hire cautious people. We hire people who can operate at the speed of the company — which is to say, faster than the situation has been fully explained.

If you have shipped something before it was ready, led a project before the brief existed, or deployed a decision before the stakeholders were aligned — you may already be one of us.

We do not require QA experience. We do not reward hesitation. We do not hold retrospectives until after the second deployment.

Operating Values

How we work.

01
Deploy, then understand.

Understanding is a post-launch activity. The market will clarify what the product means once it has been exposed to real conditions.

02
Speed compounds. Caution doesn't.

Every cycle saved in review becomes a deployment advantage. The organization that ships first owns the frame.

03
Consensus is a post-launch metric.

We deploy, and alignment forms around the outcome. We do not wait for it to form before we begin.
